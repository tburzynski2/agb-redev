Custom Garage Builders in WisconsinPrecision From Concept to Creation

American Garage Builders in Wisconsin brings your custom garage vision to life using advanced technology and expert craftsmanship. We offer custom, prefabricated and detached garages. Our unique, vertically integrated approach includes engineering our own stamped trusses, gables and dormers in our in-house framing mill. This process yields exceptional quality through precision and engine-controlled milling while providing cost savings for our customers. Every garage we build meets the highest standards and is backed by our experienced, local contractors. Contact us today for a free custom garage estimate!

Our Garage Options

Prefab Garages +

Our prefabricated garages offer quality and value. We design each garage digitally to ensure structural integrity before construction begins. Built in our controlled facility, your prefab garage benefits from precision cutting and assembly, free from weather-related issues. Our bulk material purchasing means you get a premium structure at a competitive price.

Detached Garages +

As a premier detached garage builder, we leverage advanced technology and controlled manufacturing for superior results. Our process includes computer-aided design, laser-guided cutting and automated assembly.

Custom Garages +

Your custom garage is engineered for excellence from the ground up. We start with your unique design, transform it into a detailed digital model and analyze it for structural integrity.

Garage Windows

American's garage windows feature tension sealing and a self-flashing design for easy installation and weather-tight performance. Available in two sizes with single-hung operation, they include removable screens and built-in "J" construction ideal for vinyl siding. The windows require no painting and come with a security lock, making them both practical and secure for garage applications.
