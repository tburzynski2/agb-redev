Custom Garage Builders in WisconsinPrecision From Concept to Creation

American Garage Builders in Wisconsin brings your custom garage vision to life using advanced technology and expert craftsmanship. We offer custom, prefabricated and detached garages. Our unique, vertically integrated approach includes engineering our own stamped trusses, gables and dormers in our in-house framing mill. This process yields exceptional quality through precision and engine-controlled milling while providing cost savings for our customers. Every garage we build meets the highest standards and is backed by our experienced, local contractors. Contact us today for a free custom garage estimate!
